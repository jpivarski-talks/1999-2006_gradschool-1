Fit function is

Given an energy in MeV, 

      real*8 rm, wspread, hc
      rm = mass * 1000.d0
      wspread = wide
      hc = enMeV(i) - rm
      backgr = back * 9430.d0**2 / enMeV(i)**2

      fit = area * gbwkf( rm, gam, wspread, hc ) + backgr
